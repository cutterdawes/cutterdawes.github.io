Cutter Dawes

Cutter Dawes is a recent graduate from Princeton University with a B.A. in Mathematics. His research interests include the inductive biases of artificial neural networks and developing machine learning models inspired by cognitive science. He has a background in both academic and industry research, with a focus on computational neuroscience and data analysis.
